## Client Checklist
- [ ] Get brand guidelines (colors, fonts, logo)
- [ ] Collect product photos and descriptions
- [ ] Determine number of products to showcase
- [ ] Define target audience
- [ ] Discuss e-commerce needs (view only vs. buy online)
- [ ] Get social media links
- [ ] Confirm page requirements
- [ ] Discuss special features (size charts, lookbooks, etc.)

## Discovery Questions to Ask
1. What's your brand story/mission?
2. Who is your target customer?
3. Do you need online shopping, or just product showcase?
4. How many products do you want featured?
5. Do you have existing branding (logo, colors, fonts)?
6. What's your budget and timeline?
7. Do you need size charts, product filters, or collections?
8. Any competitor sites you like?

## Potential Features
- Product grid with hover effects
- Product detail pages
- Size/fit guide
- Lookbook/Gallery
- Newsletter signup
- Social media integration
- Shopping cart (if e-commerce)
